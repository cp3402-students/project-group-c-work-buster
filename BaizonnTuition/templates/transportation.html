Transportation

Prime Academy in Upper Serangoon is easily accessible by public transport and car. The Serangoon MRT station is a 10-minute walk away, and buses 53, 58, and 100 service the area. Ample parking is available at nearby shopping centers with fee validation. Plan your route and allow extra travel time during peak hours for a smooth commute.
