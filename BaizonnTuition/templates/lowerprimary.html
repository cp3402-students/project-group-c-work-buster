Student Success Story:

Lily

O-Levels Mathematic Score: B3

More Story

Lily, a Lower Primary student, once struggled with reading. But with the support of our dedicated teachers, she blossomed into a confident reader. Lily’s journey was filled with small victories—each word mastered, every story completed. Her eyes sparkled when she read aloud in class, and her classmates cheered her on.

One day, Lily discovered a magical book about a brave adventurer. She devoured its pages, lost in a world of imagination. Inspired, she wrote her own adventure—a tale of courage, friendship, and overcoming challenges. Her teacher praised her creativity, and Lily beamed with pride.

As Lily’s confidence grew, so did her love for learning. She became a math whiz, solving puzzles faster than anyone. Science experiments fascinated her, and she dreamed of becoming a scientist. Lily’s journey at Lower Primary School transformed her from a hesitant reader into a curious explorer—a testament to the power of education and encouragement.
