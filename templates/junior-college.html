More Story

In my first year of Junior College, Physics was my most daunting subject. I found the concepts overwhelming and struggled to keep up with my classmates. My turning point came when I joined Prime Academy in 2023 and started working with Mr. Chan. His teaching style was innovative and personalized, focusing on my weaknesses and adapting lessons to suit my learning pace. He used real-world applications and interactive experiments to make complex theories more accessible.

Over time, my understanding of Physics improved dramatically. By the end of the first term, my grades had risen from a D to a C, which was a significant achievement for me. This progress motivated me to work even harder and apply the techniques Mr. Chan had taught me.

When it was time for my preliminary examinations, I was thrilled to achieve a B. This was a milestone I had not thought possible before joining Prime Academy. The confidence I gained from this success carried me through to the final A-Level exams. On the day of the Physics A-Level exam, I recalled Mr. Chan’s advice to stay calm, break down each problem, and apply the fundamental principles methodically.

When the results were finally released, I was overjoyed to see an A next to my name. This accomplishment was a testament to my hard work, perseverance, and the exceptional guidance I received from Mr. Chan. His belief in my potential and his unwavering support transformed my academic journey, allowing me to achieve results that once seemed beyond my reach.
