During Secondary 4, I faced immense challenges with Mathematics. Struggling with concepts, I often lagged behind my peers. Joining Prime Academy in 2022 changed everything. My success was the result of relentless hard work, perseverance, and exceptional guidance. Mr. Lee’s belief in my potential transformed my academic journey.
